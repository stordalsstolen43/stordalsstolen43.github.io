Komfy Cooker Guard

The Komfy cooker guard protects against dry cooking and overheating. Here is a simple guide for everyday use:

Normal Use

The cooker guard works automatically. When you turn on the stove, the guard starts monitoring the temperature. The sensor should be placed in its wall bracket above the stove.

Important Functions

  • Overtemperature Warning: If the stove gets too hot, the guard will beep 3 times every 5 seconds for 20 seconds, and the sensor will light up red. Lower the temperature immediately, or press the control button to temporarily approve the high temperature.
  • Tamper Function: If the sensor is not properly mounted in its bracket while the stove is in use, the guard will beep 5 times within 5 seconds before the stove automatically turns off. Put the sensor back in its bracket and press the control button.
  • Timer Function (Optional): The guard can start a countdown timer when it detects the stove is on. When the timer reaches zero, the stove turns off. During the last 5 minutes, the guard will issue an alarm. Press the control button to restart the timer.

Alarm Signals and Solutions

  • 1 signal every 15 seconds for 5 minutes: Timer is in the last 5 minutes. Press the control button to restart the timer.
  • 2 signals every 5 minutes: Battery replacement is necessary. Replace the batteries (3 pcs AA).
  • 2 signals and stove disconnects after 10 min: Battery replacement is necessary. Replace the batteries (3 pcs AA).
  • 3 signals every 5 seconds with solid red light: Overtemperature. Lower the temperature or press the control button.
  • 3 signals every 30 seconds: Continued overtemperature after disconnection. Lower the temperature or press the control button.
  • 5 signals within 5 seconds: Sensor is not properly mounted. Put the sensor back in its bracket and press the control button.

Emergency Mode

In case of problems you cannot solve, you can activate emergency mode which provides power to the stove for 12 hours (can be repeated). NOTE: All safety functions are deactivated during this period. Use the time to contact an electrician.

5. Important Notes and Rules

Flashlight

As it can get very dark at night, we strongly recommend bringing a flashlight.

Firewood

Wood for the indoor fireplace is under the kitchen window. Wood for the outdoor fireplace is stored at the back (north end) of the cabin.

Waste Disposal

Please dispose of all waste in the designated containers located at the car park just off the main road.

Hiking/Fishing

If you plan on hiking, please bring hiking boots. Please be aware that a fishing license may be required for fishing in local waters.
